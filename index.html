Introducing QuickBite Summarizer

  • QuickBite Summarizer is an article/text summarizer optimized for smartphones.
  • Share content with QuickBite Summarizer on WhatsApp to get a summary in less than a minute (usually within seconds).
  • Check out the video below for a quick tutorial.

Video Overview

Features

  • AI-Powered Summaries: Generates summaries using the ChatGPT API.
  • Versatile Summarization: Can summarize various types of content, including :
    • Web pages
    • Tweets
    • News articles
    • Blog posts
    • Capable of summarizing anything with a URL

How to use

  1. Step 1: Share the content you want to summarise with the QuickBite Summarizer WhatsApp account using your smartphone's sharing feature.
  2. Step 2: Receive the summary within seconds (up to a minute).
  3. Step 3: Boom! Enjoy your QuickBite summary.
