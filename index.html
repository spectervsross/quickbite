Introducing QuickBite Summarizer

QuickBite Summarizer is an article/text summarizer optimized for smartphones. Share content with QuickBite Summarizer on WhatsApp to get a summary in less than a minute (usually within seconds).

Video Overview

Features

  • AI-Powered Summaries: Generates summaries using the ChatGPT API.
  • Versatile Summarization: Can summarize various types of content, including:
    • Web pages
    • Tweets
    • News articles
    • Blog posts
    • Capable of summarizing anything with a URL

How to use

  1. Step 1: Share content with QuickBite Summarizer on WhatsApp using your phone's share feature.
  2. Step 2: Receive the summary within seconds (up to a minute).
  3. Step 3: Boom! Enjoy your QuickBite summary.
